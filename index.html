Pencilon is a full-service advertising agency based in Malappuram, Kerala — built for brands that refuse to blend in.

We craft brand identities that define who you are, run digital campaigns that reach the right people, and produce social media creatives — brochures, e-posters, stories and reels — that make people stop scrolling.

Every pixel is placed with purpose. Every idea starts with a pencil — and that pencil is always on.
